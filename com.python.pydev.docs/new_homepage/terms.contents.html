Prices and Terms of Sale

Pydev Extensions licenses are sold on a per-developer basis, meaning that it is valid for a single developer to use on any number of machines.

Pydev Extensions has a fixed price of $40 (US Dollars), but discounts are available (on request) when buying more than 10 licenses.

Upgrading

Buying Pydev includes free access to all the releases within one year of the original purchase date. Subsequent renewals will be available for half of the full product price.
