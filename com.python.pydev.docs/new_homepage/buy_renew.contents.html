How to renew and how much does it cost?

Pydev Extensions licenses:

  • Per-developer: valid for a single developer to use on any number of machines
  • Multi-developer: may be installed only on a fixed number of machines, but can be used by any number of developers.
  • Site-license: may be used by all the developers from a company site.
  • Worldwide-license: may be used by all the developers from a company.


Prices (in US Dollars):

  • For each developer on a single or multi-developer license renewal: $29.75
  • For one site-license renewal: $490.00
  • For one worldwide-license renewal: $2100.00


How does the renewal work?

For each license you bought, you can buy a renewal for a license of the same type.

If you want to upgrade your license to a site-license in the renewal process, you can have a discount of $12.75 for each license you are renewing (the discount is then applied on the full price of a site license).

E.g.: If you have a 10 multi-user license, you can have a $127.5 discount ($12.75 * 10) from the site license price, so, upgrading to the site license in this case would cost $572.5 ($700.0 - 127.5)

Please e-mail fabioz.pydev at gmail.com for more details on how to upgrade your license in the renewal process.


What does the license provide?

All renewed licenses are valid for one year, and include free access to all the releases within that time frame (the license may no longer be used after that time). Subsequent renewals will be available with a discount of 30% of the full product price (at the time of the renewal).

Also, check the Pydev Extensions license to know what you get when you buy it.


How to renew?

Just click the button that matches your payment choice (in the right panel) and follow its instructions. If you enter more than 1 item in the quantity, you will automatically receive a renewal for a multi-user license.

NOTE: Your license will be sent to the e-mail you specify (at paypal or share-it), which does not necessarily need to match your previous e-mail (in this case, you have to specify your old e-mail when you buy it if it changed).


Now that I bought it, how do I get my license?

After your payment is complete, you should receive your license within 2 business days in your e-mail (altough it usually takes just some hours).

  • After receiving your license, go to the Pydev Extensions preferences (window > Preferences > Pydev > Pydev Extensions):




  • Fill in the e-mail field with the same e-mail you received your renewal. And paste the new license you received.

Important: The e-mail you specify must be the same e-mail you used to make your payment (through Paypal or share-it) in the renewal.
