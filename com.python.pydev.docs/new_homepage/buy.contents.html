Now that I bought it, how do I get my license?

After your payment is complete, follow the instructions below to get your license:

  • Go to the Pydev Extensions preferences (window > Preferences > Pydev > Pydev Extensions):




  • Press the 'Request license' button, and the following screen will appear:


    • PUT IMAGE!!!


  • Fill in your name and e-mail, press 'Get Information' and then 'Send Information'. When you do that, an e-mail will be sent with the information you provided. After 2 days, you should receive your license through the e-mail you specified.

Important: The e-mail you specify must be the same e-mail you used to make your payment through Paypal.
