How does it come and how much does it cost?

Pydev Extensions licenses are sold either on a per-developer (it is valid for a single developer to use on any number of machines), or on a multi-developer basis (it may be installed only on a fixed number of machines, but can be used by any number of developers).

Pydev Extensions has a fixed price of $35 (US Dollars), but discounts are available when buying more than 10 licenses.

Upgrading

Buying Pydev includes free access to all the releases within one year of the original purchase date. Subsequent renewals will be available with a discount of 30% of the full product price (at the time of the renewal).

Also, check the Pydev Extensions license to know what you get when you buy it.

How to buy?

Just click the button below (paypal) and follow its instructions.

Now that I bought it, how do I get my license?

After your payment is complete, you should receive your license within 2 business days in your e-mail (altough it usually takes just some hours).

  • After receiving your license, Go to the Pydev Extensions preferences (window > Preferences > Pydev > Pydev Extensions):




  • Fill in the e-mail field with the same e-mail you received your license. And paste the license you received.

Important: The e-mail you specify must be the same e-mail you used to make your payment through Paypal.
