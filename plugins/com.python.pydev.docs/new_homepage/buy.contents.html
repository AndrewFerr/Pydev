How does it come and how much does it cost?

Pydev Extensions licenses:

  • Per-developer: valid for a single developer to use on any number of machines
  • Multi-developer: may be installed only on a fixed number of machines, but can be used by any number of developers.
  • Site-license: may be used by all the developers from a company site.
  • Worldwide-license: may be used by all the developers from a company.


Prices (in US Dollars):

  • For each developer on a single or multi-developer license: $42.50 and a renewal price of $29.75
  • For one site-license: $700.00 and a renewal price of $490.00
  • For one worldwide-license: $3000.00 and a renewal price of $2100.00


What does the license provide?

All licenses are valid for one year, and include free access to all the releases within that time frame (the license may no longer be used after that time). Subsequent renewals will be available with a discount of 30% of the full product price (at the time of the renewal).

Also, check the Pydev Extensions license to know what you get when you buy it.


How to buy?

Just click the button that matches your payment choice (in the right panel) and follow its instructions. If you enter more than 1 item in the quantity, you will automatically receive a multi-user license.

NOTE: Your license will be sent to the e-mail you specify (at paypal or share-it). If you wish to buy it as a 'gift' for someone else, please contact me at 'fabioz.pydev at gmail.com' along with the complete name and e-mail of the person that should receive the license (but please, do that before you buy the license and wait for the confirmation that your e-mail was received).


Now that I bought it, how do I get my license?

After your payment is complete, you should receive your license within 2 business days in your e-mail (altough it usually takes just some hours).

  • After receiving your license, go to the Pydev Extensions preferences (window > Preferences > Pydev > Pydev Extensions):




  • Fill in the e-mail field with the same e-mail you received your license. And paste the license you received.

Important: The e-mail you specify must be the same e-mail you used to make your payment (through Paypal or share-it).
